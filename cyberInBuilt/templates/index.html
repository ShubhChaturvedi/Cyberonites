"Cybersecurity is a subject that requires logic, knowledge, thought and commitment."- Ian R. McAndrew" Cyberonites is the official cyber security club of GLA University. It enables the members to enhance and develop knowledge and skills in various aspects of cyber security such as network security, penetration testing, cryptography and many more.... It aims to enhance and sustain a fundamentally secure environment for IT based administration in organization by enhancing knowledge and designing policies in the field of cyber security. Our club work under mentorship of Mr. Asheesh Sir, Assistant professor under CEA Department, He is a passionate professor for the subject, teaching and learning, with majestic thoughts and helpful nature. Seniors are always cooperative and encourage us to be more productive and efficient. Our club organize workshops/seminars to lead better understanding of cyber security technology and adoption that can help consolidate and synthesize existing resources and best practices to support policy makers’ ability to understand and implement cyber security measures that work best for the organization.
